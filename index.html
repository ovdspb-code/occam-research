About CRN

Coherent Resonant Netting (CRN) is a two-stage decision architecture where low-amplitude wave-like exploration prunes hypothesis space at low metabolic cost, followed by high-gain spiking commitment. The theory predicts that intermediate noise (dephasing) optimizes selective signal routing ‚Äî the inverted-U transport window, or ENAQT. These interactive demos let you explore this phenomenon on real biological connectomes.
